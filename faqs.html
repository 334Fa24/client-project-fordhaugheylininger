Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? Look through our FAQ's to see if it's been answered.

What services do you offer?

We offer a range of grooming services, including baths, haircuts, nail trims, ear cleaning, teeth brushing, de-shedding treatments, flea and tick treatments, and more. We also have special packages like puppy grooming and deluxe spa options for pampering your pet!

How often should my dog be groomed?

The frequency of grooming depends on your dog's breed, coat type, and lifestyle. Dogs with long or curly coats often need grooming every 4-6 weeks, while short-haired breeds may only need a bath and nail trim every 8-12 weeks. Regular grooming keeps your dog healthy, comfortable, and looking great.

How long does a grooming session take?

A typical grooming session lasts between 2 to 3 hours, depending on the size of your dog, the condition of their coat, and the services selected. For more complex services like de-shedding or specific breed cuts, it might take longer. We always strive to provide efficient service while ensuring your dog feels comfortable and relaxed.

Do I need to make an appointment in advance?

Yes, we recommend booking your appointment in advance, especially during busy times like holidays or weekends. You can schedule appointments easily through our website or by calling us directly.

Do you offer mobile grooming services?

Yes! We offer mobile grooming services where we bring the salon to you. Our fully equipped grooming van provides the same high-quality services at the convenience of your home. Mobile grooming is perfect for dogs who may be stressed by car rides or busy environments.

What products do you use?

We use high-quality, eco-friendly, and natural grooming products that are safe for your dog’s coat and skin. If your dog has sensitive skin or allergies, we offer hypoallergenic and medicated shampoos.

Can I stay with my dog during the grooming session?

For the safety and comfort of both your dog and our groomers, we typically request that pet parents do not stay during the session. Many dogs are calmer and less anxious when their owners are not present. However, feel free to discuss any concerns with us, and we can make accommodations if needed.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that plans change, so we kindly ask for at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours’ notice may be subject to a fee.

What are your payment options?

We accept cash, credit cards, and mobile payment options like Apple Pay and Google Pay. Payments can be made after your dog’s grooming session is completed.
