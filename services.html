Our Services

Furry Fresh Grooming offers a variety of services. From full service baths to a simple nail trim, we've got everything you need for a clean pup.

Basic Bath and Brush

  • Includes a gentle shampoo bath, brushing, and blowdry.
  • Nail trim, ear cleaning, and a spritz of cologne.

Full Grooming

  • Includes everything in the basic bath and brush.
  • Haircut or breed-specific styling by professional groomers.

Puppy's First Groom

  • Gentle Introduction to grooming for puppies.
  • Light bath, brushing, and nail trim.

De-Shedding Treatment

  • Specialized treatment to reduce shedding.
  • Helps minimize shedding at home and keeps coats healthy.

Deluxe Spa Package

  • Includes Full Grooming and deep conditioning, paw balm, blueberry facial, and a fashionable bow or bandana.

Add On's

  • Teeth brushing
  • Flea and tick treatment
  • Special skin treatments for sensitive dogs.
  • Paw and nose moisturizing.

Pet Bakery

Freshly baked, dog-friendly treats available for purchase.

Grooming Boutique

High-quality grooming tools, stylish collars, and pet accessories.

Doggie Daycare

While your pup is being groomed, they can enjoy supervised playtime
